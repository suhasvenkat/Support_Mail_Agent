BILLING SUPPORT GUIDE

Common billing issues and resolutions:

1. Double charges: Verify transaction IDs. Refund duplicate within 3-5 business days.
2. Incorrect invoice amount: Review contract terms. Issue corrected invoice within 24 hours.
3. Payment not reflected: Check bank transfer reference. Update account within 2 business days.
4. Unexpected charges: Review subscription plan. Remove unauthorized charges immediately.
5. Refund requests: Process within 5-7 business days to original payment method.
6. Invoice not received: Resend invoice to registered email within 1 hour.
7. Billing address update: Update within 24 hours. Effective from next billing cycle.
8. Promotional discount not applied: Verify promo code. Apply credit to next invoice.
