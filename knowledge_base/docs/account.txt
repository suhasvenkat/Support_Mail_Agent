ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT GUIDE

Account support procedures:

1. Password reset: Click forgot password. Reset link valid for 24 hours.
2. Account locked: Unlock after 3 failed attempts via email verification.
3. Email change: Verify new email address. Update takes 24 hours.
4. Account closure: Download data first. Deletion permanent after 30 days.
5. Two-factor authentication: Enable via security settings. Use authenticator app.
6. User permissions: Admin can modify roles. Changes effective immediately.
7. Account upgrade: Select new plan. Prorated charge applied immediately.
8. Data export: Request via settings. Download link sent within 2 hours.
