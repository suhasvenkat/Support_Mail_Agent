REFUND POLICY GUIDE

Refund eligibility and process:

1. Standard refunds: Processed within 5-7 business days to original payment method.
2. Duplicate charge refund: Immediate processing upon verification of duplicate.
3. Service downtime credit: SLA credit applied within 2 billing cycles.
4. Cancelled subscription: Pro-rated refund for unused period if cancelled within 30 days.
5. Wrong item delivered: Full refund or replacement within 24 hours of report.
6. Damaged goods: Photo evidence required. Full refund processed within 48 hours.
7. Refund status check: Provide transaction ID and email for status update.
8. Non-refundable items: Digital downloads and custom orders are non-refundable.
