GENERAL SUPPORT GUIDE

General customer support procedures:

1. Response times: Email 24 hours, chat 5 minutes, phone immediate during business hours.
2. Escalation process: Tier 1 → Tier 2 → Manager within 24 hours if unresolved.
3. Service status: Check status.ourservice.com for real-time system status.
4. Business hours: Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM EST. Emergency support 24/7.
5. Feedback submission: Use feedback form in app. Response within 3 business days.
6. Feature requests: Submit via product portal. Reviewed in monthly sprint.
7. Compliance queries: GDPR data requests processed within 30 days.
8. Partnership inquiries: Forward to partnerships@ourservice.com.
