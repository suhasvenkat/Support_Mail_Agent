SHIPPING AND DELIVERY GUIDE

Delivery support procedures:

1. Order tracking: Use tracking number on carrier website for real-time updates.
2. Delayed delivery: Contact carrier after 3 business days past expected date.
3. Missing package: File carrier claim within 7 days. Replacement shipped within 48 hours.
4. Wrong item received: Keep item. Correct item shipped within 24 hours free of charge.
5. Damaged delivery: Photo evidence required. Replacement or refund within 48 hours.
6. Address change: Only possible before shipment. Contact support immediately.
7. International shipping: Customs delays normal. Allow 5-10 additional business days.
8. Delivery signature required: Reschedule via carrier portal or collect from depot.
